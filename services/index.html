Headlight Restoration


Dr. Headlight

Majestic Car Care is the exclusive dealer for Doctor Headlight’s patented headlight restoration process. It is the only system that guarantees to restore any failed headlight to 100% like brand new, regardless of its current condition.

Doctor Headlight’s system is not a cleaner; it is a restoration process that goes beyond all current conventional polishing and kit based cleaning processes to address issues that were previously impossible to correct. Rock chips, sand pitting, dry cracks, oxidation, tarnishing, internal water damage, internal HID haze, failed seals, and internal condensation can now all be addressed within one restoration appointment.

Doctor Headlight creates a protective layer over your headlights and protects it from sand pitting, and avoids oxidation and yellowing because of prolonged exposure to damaging UV rays.

Our Services
  • Cosmetic Repairs: When headlights experience more than average wear and tear such as chips from rocks, paint scuffs from accidents, and other minor, repairable damage, Doctor Headlight is here to provide a cost-effective alternative to purchasing new headlights. Our cosmetic repair service will have your headlights looking brand new again.
  • Oxidation Removal: When the UV coating on your headlights wears down over the years, that yellow, cloudy, foggy appearance you see is oxidation. Doctor Headlight removes the oxidation from your headlights allowing your lights to shine brighter and provide more safety when you drive.
  • Tarnishing Removal: Doctor Headlight restores tarnished headlights to like-new condition, even if the tarnishing is inside the headlight lens.
  • Seal Repair: Repairing a failed seal will eliminate the need for replacement due to water entering the headlight assembly. Let Doctor Headlight protect your headlights from future water damage by getting your headlight seals repaired.
  • Condensation Removal: Condensation can reduce a headlight’s output by up to 75%. Doctor Headlight’s process eliminates 99.9% of the headlight’s internal moisture, restoring it to like-new condition.
  • Water Extraction: Doctor Headlight can remove water that has collected within your headlight. This will prevent damage to your vehicle’s electrical system as well as keep you from having to purchase new headlights.
  • Smoked Headlights: Doctor Headlight can add style to your vehicle, without compromising safety, with our smoked headlight installations.
  • Smoked Taillights: Doctor Headlight also offers another highly sought-after vehicle upgrade, smoked taillights.

Windshield Repair

Windshields are easily damaged by flying debris resulting in glass chips, glass bullseye’s or star-breaks. If the windshields are left untreated then this level of minor damage can “creep” quickly throughout the whole windshield leading to a major repair or complete windshield replacement. Our windshield repair technology invisibly seals the damage seamlessly and restoring the windshield back to its original strength. Using the latest glass repair equipment and techniques our specialist Smart Repair Technicians based in Dubai can make quick work of minor windscreen damage.
