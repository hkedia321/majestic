Smart Power Cut

Power Cut Compound is a premium Acu-Bead compound with superior finishing ability. As a body shop safe (silicone & wax free,) Power Cut is the most powerful polishing compound. Power Cut will remove many moderate to heavy surface imperfections and 900-1500 grit sand scratches, swirls, scuff marks and webbing fast. As buffing continues, its unique Acu-Bead abrasive system breaks down into fine tiny particles that eventually become a micro-polish. This metamorphosis enables the professional user to compound and polish delivering a superior finish in one easy step.
