SmartSealant


If durability, protection and extreme shine are important to you, you’re going to love SmartSealant. SmartSealant’s new formulation of detergent resistant polymers blended with pure Brazilian #1 Grade Ivory Carnauba Wax and natural oils create a paint care treatment with incredible shine, depth, slickness and lasting protection. Automotive paint is under attack every hour it is parked outside. Extra-strength UV sunscreen protection makes SmartSealant the ideal choice for vehicles exposed to the sun for long durations. With regular use, SmartSealant prevents fading and UV induced oxidation and seals out corrosive elements like salt water, acid rain and factory pollutants.

RimWax


RimWax – provides extreme shine and protection for all wheel finishes including chrome and alloy wheels. Don’t let dull, dirty rims ruin the look of your freshly washed and waxed car! RimWax is a specialized cleaner, polish and wax that quickly restores a bright, polished and protected wheel finish. The anti-static RimWax formula actually helps repel brake dust to reduce dust buildup by as much as 75%. The fine RimWax polish brightens all chrome, polished aluminum and factory clear coat finish wheels. The ultra-tough RimWax sealant protects wheels for months, preventing water spotting and staining from road contaminates and brake dust buildup.
