SmartDressing


SmartDressing is a non-greasy dry to the touch premium conditioner that is highly versatile and provides an outstanding long lasting shine and protection to all tires, rubber, and vinyl and leather surfaces. SmartDressing is a water based formula that leaves surfaces shiny, protected, and absolutely dry to the touch. The rich pH balanced formula penetrates beyond the surface to rejuvenate and maintain pliability and integrity. SmartDressing is also formulated with UV blocking agents to help keep surfaces from cracking, fading, and hardening while defending them against the harmful rays of the sun.Simply wipe on and walk away. If desired, wipe the finish dry for a low gloss, satin finish. For more gloss, apply a second coat. Its that easy!

SmartGel


This unique formula of Smartgel quickly penetrates rubber trim and tires to hydrate and provide long lasting protection. Simply wipe on using a pad to restore the look of faded tires, trim, bumpers, seals and stripping that provides a shine that lasts for months. SmartGel is a non streak, no run and non drip formula that works to repel water and detergents and helps keep tire walls hydrated and supple as well as looking new.
